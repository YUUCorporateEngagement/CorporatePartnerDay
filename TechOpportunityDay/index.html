About the Event

What is Tech Up Opportunity Day?

Tech Up Opportunity Day is a half-day event hosted by Jobs First NYC Tech Sector Network in partnership with Year Up United, designed to connect motivated individuals from our pipeline with accredited tech training programs and aligned employers in New York City.

Attendees are career changers, first-generation professionals, and recent graduates who are actively exploring how to break into the tech sector. They come ready to learn, ready to engage, and ready to take next steps.

The event combines expert panels, hands-on skill building, and a structured Training Organization Fair designed to help participants move from interest to action on the same day.

1:00 – 4:00 PM · Tuesday, June 10, 2026 · 85 Broad Street, 6th Floor

Event Flow

The Day at a Glance

Programming is designed to move attendees through a thoughtful arc: community building, sector knowledge, practical skill development, and direct engagement with training options.

12:30 PM
Staff & Organization Arrival
Setup time for participating organizations and Year Up United team
1:00 PM
Arrival, Registration & Light Refreshments
Attendees arrive, check in, and network informally
1:30 PM
Welcome, Objectives & Networking Warm-Up
Speed networking to build community and set the tone for the day
1:50 PM
Alumni & Employer Panel
Program alumni and employer partners share career pathways, advice, and what it really takes to break into tech in New York
2:30 PM
Skill-building workshop: Opportunity Ready - A guide to navigating career fairs, networking events, and beyond
Attendees practice their elevator pitch, learn what questions to ask, and role-play table conversations before heading to the fair
3:00 PM
🌟 Training Organization Fair
Attendees visit each organization's table, complete networking activity guide, and log programs of interest in their trackers. Your team is live, staffing your table, and collecting warm leads.
3:45 PM
Wrap-Up, Feedback & Incentive Distribution
Following the event, participants will receive clear next steps including a resource landing page with your organization's information. Your team will receive a link to share feedback about the event and track outcomes.
Partner Requirements

What We Ask of Participating Organizations

To ensure the best experience for attendees and a smooth day of programming, participating organizations must meet the following criteria and fulfill the following commitments.

01

Active Recruitment Window

Your organization must be actively recruiting or accepting applications within three months of the event date (June 10, 2026). Attendees will leave ready to act, so your pipeline needs to be open.

02

Program Information Submission by Deadline

Submit your program details, including areas of focus, eligibility requirements, program length, certifications offered, and your application or next-steps link by the deadline provided. This content will be used to build the post-event resource page for attendees.

03

Live Registration Link

Provide a working link for real-time registration or application that will be accessible to attendees at your table via QR code and included in post-event follow-up.

04

Attend the Pre-Event Prep Call

A representative from your organization is required to join our pre-event coordination call. Details will be shared after registration is confirmed. This call covers logistics, expectations, and day-of flow.

05

Follow-Up Tracking

After the event, participating organizations are asked to track follow-up with leads from the day using Year Up United's provided tracker form. This helps us measure impact and improve future events.
